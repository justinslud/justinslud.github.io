Inbox zero.

How to do it: 1. Unsubscribe 2. Don't subscribe to new things 3. Delete old emails

Unsubscribe

Emails should have an "Unsubscribe Me" or similar link at the bottom of the email. Sometimes they are hidden or in small, faded text.

Spam or Block

You can move the message to the Spam folder. Similarly, you can select options on the message and Block Sender.

Unroll

10minutemail

Do you want to get some free book ...? Some forms don't even send the link to your email, so you can give any email. Most forms do send the link to your email. For these, use 10minutemail.com as a temporary email. This could come back to bite me in the future.
