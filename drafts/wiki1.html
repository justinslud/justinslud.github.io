Understanding what we want to scrape

Here's an example snapshot from November 11, 2018:

Each page contains all headlines from a month, but there's also other information such as recent deaths, ... But for now, we want to focus on headlines.

Here is all the data worth collecting from the webpage:

  • day
  • month
  • year
  • subject (Sports, Business and Economy, etc)
  • event () if it exists
  • text: the actual headline

Looking at the example, most headlines have a source name enclosed in parenthesis at the end. We can collect those now and extract them later.
